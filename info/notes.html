Plantagenet: Implementation Notes

Currently all the changes from rulebook to RTT :

  • One side does all of Feed, Pillage and Disband before next side.
  • King player Feed, Pillage, and Disband first after his command card.
  • One side does both Waste and Discard before next side.
  • Loser does all their Ending the Battle steps before the victor.
  • A side cannot intercept an enemy if the enemy already has lords there.
  • Suspicion counts printed influence, not modified.
  • You can only play Parliament's Truce and Blocked Ford during your own time to play. You cannot play Parliament's Truce after the enemy declared an interception but before rolling.
  • Caltrops is attached to an engagement.
