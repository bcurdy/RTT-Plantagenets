Plantagenet: Implementation Notes

Sequence of play changes for expediency:

  • One side does all Pay steps before next side.
  • One side does all of Feed, Pillage and Disband before next side.
  • The King side Feeds first after their command card.
  • You cannot play Parliament's Truce after the enemy has declared an interception.

Other minor rules changes:

  • You cannot Intercept a locale which has enemy non-moving Lords.
  • Suspicion counts printed influence, not modified.
  • Caltrops is attached to a friendly Lord.
